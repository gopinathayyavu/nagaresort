Our Story

From Pugal Holidays to Naga Resort – a journey of transformation

Legacy Reinvented

What was once known as Pugal Holidays and later Pumo Resort has been reimagined into Naga Resort – a sanctuary where luxury meets the wild beauty of Kodaikanal. We’ve preserved the soul of the original retreat while elevating every detail to offer a world‑class hill station experience.

Our name, "Naga", pays homage to the serpentine mountain streams and the mystical energy of the Western Ghats. Every stay is designed to reconnect you with nature, without compromising on modern comforts.

Our Philosophy

Nature First

We build around trees, not over them. Our architecture blends with the landscape, and every window frames a piece of Kodaikanal’s magic.

Thoughtful Luxury

Soft linens, curated amenities, and personalised service – luxury that feels warm, never ostentatious.

Local Heartbeat

We collaborate with local artisans, source regional produce, and celebrate Tamil hospitality in every interaction.

The Journey

1998

Pugal Holidays

Opened as a cosy family-run guesthouse, welcoming travellers to Kodaikanal’s pristine hills.

2015

Pumo Resort

Expanded into a full‑service resort, adding cottages and a restaurant with valley views.

2025

Naga Resort

A complete transformation – luxury redesign, eco‑initiatives, and a renewed promise of serenity.
